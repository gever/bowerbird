device set-up

All satellite tracking devices must be set up properly in order for the system to work. Pilots should do this before arriving at the competition. From your pilot page (accessed by clicking on the tile with your pilot number) click on Help: Tracking Device Setup. OR go to http://bbtrack.me/pilothelp/### (replacing ### with your 3 or 4 digit pilot number).

status codes
